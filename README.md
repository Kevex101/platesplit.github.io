# Privacy Policy for PlateSplit

**Effective date:** March 28, 2026

PlateSplit is a bill-splitting app that helps users scan receipts, review items, split totals among people, and save or share summaries.

## 1. Information We Collect

PlateSplit is designed to work without account creation.

Most receipt, item, and split data is stored locally on your device.

Depending on how you use the app, the following information may be accessed or processed:

- **Camera data** when you scan a receipt
- **Photo library data** when you choose a receipt image from your library
- **Receipt and bill information** such as restaurant name, items, prices, tips, people, and saved summaries
- **Purchase information** related to PlateSplit Pro, processed through Apple
- **Advertising-related and diagnostic data** collected by third-party advertising/consent providers used in the app

## 2. How We Use Information

PlateSplit uses information to:

- scan and process receipts
- let you review and edit items
- split bills among people
- save receipt history on your device
- generate and share summaries
- process and restore PlateSplit Pro purchases
- display banner ads in the free version
- support app performance, diagnostics, and advertising functions through third-party services

## 3. Data Storage

PlateSplit primarily stores bill and receipt data locally on your device using Apple technologies.

We do not require you to create an account to use the app.

If you delete the app, locally stored app data may also be deleted, subject to your device and backup settings.

## 4. Camera and Photo Access

PlateSplit may request access to:

- **Camera**, to scan receipts
- **Photo Library**, to import receipt images

You can deny or revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

## 5. In-App Purchases

PlateSplit Pro is offered as an in-app purchase through Apple StoreKit.

Purchases, billing, and related payment information are handled by Apple and are subject to Apple’s own terms and privacy practices.

## 6. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The free version of PlateSplit may show banner ads.

PlateSplit uses third-party services such as:

- **Google AdMob**
- **Google User Messaging Platform**

These services may collect certain information, such as device identifiers, IP address, diagnostics, performance data, advertising data, and interaction data, to support advertising, consent management, analytics, and service performance.

These third parties may use data according to their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## 7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

However, third-party providers used in the app, such as Apple and Google, may process data as needed to provide purchases, advertising, consent, analytics, diagnostics, or related services.

## 8. Data Retention

Locally stored bill and receipt data remains on your device until you delete it or remove the app.

Data processed by third-party providers is subject to their own retention practices and policies.

## 9. Children’s Privacy

PlateSplit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Your Choices

You can:

- choose not to grant camera or photo access
- remove saved receipts from within the app
- delete the app and its locally stored data
- manage ad/privacy permissions through device settings or consent prompts where available

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted at this same URL with a revised effective date.
